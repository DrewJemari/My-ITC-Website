Agricultural Resources

The Philippines is an archipelago nation in Southeast Asia, renowned for its rich marine resources. With over 7,000 islands and an extensive coastline, the country boasts a remarkable diversity of marine ecosystems, including:

Rice Paddies

Rice paddies are flooded fields where rice is grown. They are the most common type of agricultural land use in the Philippines.

Cornfields

Cornfields are fields where corn is grown. They are typically located in upland areas.

Sugarcane Plantations

Sugarcane plantations are large fields where sugarcane is grown. They are typically located in lowland areas.

Coconut Grooves

Coconut groves are areas where coconut trees are grown. They are typically located in coastal areas.

Banana Plantations

Banana plantations are large fields where bananas are grown. They are typically located in lowland areas.

Pineapple Plantations

Pineapple plantations are large fields where pineapples are grown. They are typically located in upland areas.
