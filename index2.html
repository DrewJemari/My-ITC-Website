Mineral Resources

The Philippines is a country blessed with abundant mineral resources, contributing significantly to its economic development and global standing. These resources, including gold, copper, nickel, chromite, and iron ore, are found across the archipelago, primarily in the island groups of Luzon, Mindanao, and Visayas.

Ophiolite Complexes

These are fragments of oceanic crust that have been thrust onto the continental plate, often containing copper, nickel, and chromite deposits.

Island Arc Volcanism

Volcanic activities have resulted in the formation of porphyry copper deposits, gold-bearing epithermal veins, and skarn deposits of gold, copper, and molybdenum.

Ultramafic Rocks

These rocks, often found in ophiolite complexes, host nickel and chromite deposits.
