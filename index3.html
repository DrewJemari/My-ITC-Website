Water Resources

The Philippines is a country blessed with abundant water resources, a vital asset for its people, economy, and environment. These resources, encompassing both surface and groundwater, play a crucial role in sustaining life, supporting agriculture, and powering industries.

Rivers

The country boasts over 420 major river basins. These rivers provide water for drinking, irrigation, hydropower generation, and transportation.

Lakes

The Philippines has over 1,500 lakes. These lakes serve as important sources of water for various purposes, including irrigation, fishing, and tourism.

Coastal Waters

The Philippines' extensive coastline provides access to rich marine resources. These marine resources contribute significantly to the country's food security and economy.
