Renewable Energy Resources

The Philippines is blessed with an abundance of renewable energy resources, offering great potential to reduce its reliance on fossil fuels and transition towards a more sustainable energy future. These resources include geothermal energy, hydropower, solar energy, wind energy, and biomass energy.

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy is harnessed from the heat generated deep within the Earth's crust and is considered a reliable and clean source of energy.

Hydropower

Hydropower, the energy derived from flowing water, is another significant renewable energy source. The country has an installed hydropower projects located in Luzon and Mindanao.

Solar Energy

The Philippines receives abundant sunlight throughout the year, making solar energy a promising renewable energy source with a growing number of solar installations.

Wind Energy

The Philippines also has a good potential for wind energy, particularly in coastal areas and mountainous regions.

Biomass Energy

Biomass energy is generated from organic matter that is primarily used for small-scale applications, such as cooking and power generation in rural areas.
